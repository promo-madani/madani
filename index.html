About Aamir Patni

AI specialist, educator, creative director, and author—helping people build practical skills for the future.

APAI SPECIALIST

10+Practical AI courses

Aamir Patni is an AI Specialist, Creative Director, AI Educator, and Content Creator based in Pakistan. With a background in Software Engineering and practical experience in app development, digital media, and artificial intelligence, he focuses on making AI accessible, practical, and useful for students, professionals, educators, creators, and businesses.

He specializes in Generative AI, Prompt Engineering, AI Content Creation, AI Image & Video Generation, AI Automation, and practical AI workflows. His work and training explore leading AI platforms and tools including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, NotebookLM, and emerging creative AI technologies.

As an AI educator, Aamir focuses on hands-on learning rather than theory alone, helping people understand how to integrate AI into real-world workflows, improve productivity, create professional content, and develop future-ready skills.

He is also the author of three Urdu-language books: AI for Kids, Facebook Marketing Complete Course, and Google Marketing Complete Course.

Through his courses, educational content, creative projects, and AI awareness initiatives, Aamir's mission is to help people confidently understand and use artificial intelligence—not simply as a tool, but as a practical skill for the future.
